Title: The Phoenix's Return
Description: A skilled healer saves a desperate woman from her abusive husband, unaware that a jade pendant will reconnect her to the palace and a forgotten past.

EXT. COURTYARD GATE - DAY

YUN'ER (O.S.)
Open the door, Yun'er!

YUN'ER frantically pounds on a tightly shut wooden door.

A fierce-looking THUG charges toward her.

THUG
(roaring)
You think you can hide?

YUN'ER turns, her face draining of color.

CHANG GE steps from the shadows, her movements fluid and practiced. She flings a handful of powder.

The THUG inhales, coughs violently, then stumbles, his hands clawing at his neck and arms.

CHANG GE
My own recipe. Feels like wasps beneath the skin. The antidote is timed.

The THUG drops to his knees, gasping.

THUG
(hoarse pleading)
Make it stop!

CHANG GE
Then listen carefully. You will forget Shen Yun'er's name. You will forget this town. If I see your face again, there is no second antidote.

She tosses a wrapped leaf onto the dirt. The THUG snatches it and chews desperately.

CHANG GE
Now crawl away.

The THUG staggers off. CHANG GE watches until he's gone, her jaw tight. She turns to the door, her expression softening almost imperceptibly.

INT. COURTYARD - DAY

YUN'ER peeks out, sees the empty street, and exhales sharply.

YUN'ER
You... you truly carry such poison?

CHANG GE
Only pepper and nettle. The fear does the rest.

YUN'ER
(quietly)
I thought—

CHANG GE
(interrupting)
You thought to use me as a shield. Again.

YUN'ER
(crying, voice cracking)
He's worse when he drinks. Look.

She pulls back her sleeve. Deep bruises pattern her arm, some old and yellowed, others fresh and dark.

YUN'ER
(touching her abdomen)
And now there's the child.

She sinks to her knees, gripping CHANG GE's hand.

YUN'ER
You promised. In the mountains, when we were barely twelve. You said if I ever needed—

EXT. WILDERNESS - DAY (WINTER)

Snow blankets the ground. Young CHANG GE, bloodied and unconscious, is sheltered by a crude lean-to. Young YUN'ER, chapped hands and hollow cheeks, presses snow to her wounds.

Young CHANG GE's eyes flutter open. She touches a rough wooden pendant at her neck.

YOUNG CHANG GE
(whisper)
You stayed.

YOUNG YUN'ER
We protect each other now.

END FLASHBACK.

INT. COURTYARD - DAY

CHANG GE stares at Yun'er. Her hand moves instinctively to her own throat, where a simple wooden pendant hangs.

CHANG GE
(softly)
The jade one is packed away. Safe.

YUN'ER
(nodding frantically)
Yes, yes. Just as you asked.

CHANG GE helps her up, decision made.

CHANG GE
Then we leave tonight. He'll return with friends.

YUN'ER
(weeping with relief)
The northern roads—

CHANG GE
Will take us to a new life. Come.

TITLE: FIVE YEARS LATER

EXT. HEALING HALL - DAY

Under the faded Healing Hall sign, TOWNSPEOPLE bow deeply.

TOWNSPEOPLE (V.O.)
Your hands have blessed this town.

INT. HEALING HALL - DAY

CHANG GE takes an elderly woman's pulse, her touch gentle.

CHANG GE
Xiao Shiliu, angelica root, two liang.

XIAO SHILIU (O.S.)
Already measured!

The boy, seven years old and quick-fingered, slides a packet onto the table.

CHANG GE
(approving)
And three liang of platycodon. You read my mind.

ELDERLY WOMAN
The boy learns your heart.

XIAO SHILIU beams.

XIAO SHILIU
Mother says healing is the only nobility that matters.

Everyone chuckles warmly.

YUN'ER enters, carrying a heavy tray. Her face is drawn, eyes hollow. She bumps an OLD MAN.

OLD MAN
Oh! Forgive me, lady—

YUN'ER
(sharp, exhausted)
Watch your step. Can't you see I'm—

She catches herself, shame flickering across her features.

CHANG GE
Yun'er. Rest.

YUN'ER
(curtly)
Rest doesn't pay our debts.

She disappears into the back room. XIAO SHILIU watches her go, his smile fading. He notices a crack in the wall panel. Prying it open, he finds a small cloth bundle.

INT. HEALING HALL - CONTINUOUS

XIAO SHILIU unwraps a carved white jade pendant, luminous and cool. He glances toward the back room, then slips it into his pocket.

INT. PAWN SHOP - DAY

XIAO SHILIU stands on his toes to reach the counter. He places the pendant down.

XIAO SHILIU
For my mother's medicine. How much?

The PAWN SHOP OWNER's breath catches. He turns the pendant over, finding a royal seal on the back.

PAWN SHOP OWNER
(carefully)
Thirty taels. For the craftsmanship.

He pushes the silver forward. XIAO SHILIU hesitates, then takes it.

INT. PALACE HALL - DAY

HIS HIGHNESS sits rigid, reviewing tax ledgers. A GUARD enters, kneeling.

GUARD
Your Highness. The pendant surfaced in a border village. With a woman matching the description. And a boy. The scholars calculate his age at seven years.

HIS HIGHNESS's hand stills. He looks up, his face a mask of old grief and sudden, terrible hope.

FADE OUT.
