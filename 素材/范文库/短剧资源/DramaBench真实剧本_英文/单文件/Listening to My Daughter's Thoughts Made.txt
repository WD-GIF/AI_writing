Title: Listening to My Daughter's Thoughts Made Me a Landlady
Description: A young mother who can hear her newborn daughter's thoughts discovers her baby has superpowers and uses them to fight back against her scheming family.

INT. LIN FAMILY VILLA - NIGHT (FLASHBACK)

A stormy night. Dark clouds cover the city sky. Rain lashes against windows.

NEWS BROADCAST (V.O.)
The National Meteorological Center warns of severe storms tonight, with potential power outages. Citizens should remain indoors while crews work to restore services.

A PREGNANT WOMAN lies in a pool of blood on the marble floor, cradling her belly. Her breathing is shallow. This is LIN MENG'S MOTHER.

PREGNANT WOMAN
(whispering)
I'll protect you... I'll bring you safely into this world...

Her hand slips. Lightning flashes.

EXT. CITY STREET - NIGHT (FLASHBACK)

An ambulance speeds through the downpour, sirens wailing.

INT. HOSPITAL DELIVERY ROOM - DAY

LIN MENG (25) lies on the delivery bed, drenched in sweat, pushing through exhaustion.

DOCTOR
One more push, Lin. You're almost there.

A newborn's cry echoes through the room.

DOCTOR
Congratulations. It's a girl.

LIN MENG collapses back, tears of relief streaming down her face as a NURSE places the baby on her chest.

INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL ROOM - DAY

LIN MENG, in a hospital gown, sits on the bed cradling her daughter LIN XIXI. She touches the baby's tiny fingers.

LIN MENG
(softly)
You chose me. And I'll be the mother you deserve.

The door swings open. LIN ZHIWEI (50s, worn suit) enters with WANG AIJUN (40s, designer handbag) and LIN YANRAN (22, smug expression).

LIN YANRAN
(leering)
Already popped out a bastard? How efficient.

LIN MENG
(eyes flashing)
My daughter is not a bastard.

WANG AIJUN
You don't even know the father's name. What would you call it?

INT. KTV PRIVATE ROOM - NIGHT

LIN MENG struggles against a SHADOWY FIGURE pinning her to a sofa.

LIN MENG
Let go! I was just delivering documents—

The scene cuts away.

FLASHBACK ENDS.

INT. PRIVATE HOSPITAL ROOM - DAY

LIN MENG
(to Lin Yanran)
You sent me there that night. You set me up.

WANG AIJUN steps forward and SNATCHES the baby from Lin Meng's arms.

LIN MENG
(frantic)
Give her back! Give her back!

LIN YANRAN slaps a document onto the bed tray.

LIN YANRAN
Sign the gift agreement for the old house, and you can keep your mistake.

LIN MENG scans the document: "Property Gift Agreement - 47 Lin Family Lane."

LIN MENG
(voice cracking)
This is all Mom left me. You're taking that too?

LIN YANRAN
Mom and I are the real Lin family now. We deserve what's ours. Right, Dad?

LIN ZHIWEI
(avoiding eye contact)
Just sign it, Meng. Keep the peace.

LIN MENG
(desperate)
You're using my daughter as leverage?

LIN YANRAN
Sign, or I drop her at the police station. Abandoned.

WANG AIJUN shakes the baby slightly. Lin Xixi begins to CRY.

LIN MENG
(breaking)
No—okay! I'll sign!

Lin Meng grabs the pen, hand shaking. She touches the paper.

LIN XIXI (V.O.)
Mommy. Don't sign. Please.

LIN MENG stops. Freezes. Looks around.

LIN MENG
...What?

LIN XIXI (V.O.)
It's me, Mommy. Listen.

LIN YANRAN
What's the hold-up? Sign!

Lin Meng stares at her daughter. Lin Xixi stops crying. Their eyes lock. Lin Meng's expression hardens.

She RIPS the document in half.

LIN MENG
No.

LIN XIXI (V.O.)
Bad woman. Hurt Mommy. Now you pay.

A faint BLUE LIGHT pulses from Lin Xixi's blanket, streaking toward Lin Yanran. Lin Yanran DOUBLES OVER, clutching her stomach.

LIN YANRAN
(gasping)
Mom—my stomach—

WANG AIJUN
What is it?

Lin Yanran GAGS, a wave of nausea hitting her. She stumbles.

LIN ZHIWEI
(stepping back)
Yanran, you're green. Get to the bathroom.

Lin Yanran sprints for the door, a hand clamped over her mouth. Lin Meng watches, eyes wide, a small smile forming.

LIN YANRAN
(at the door, voice muffled)
This isn't over!

Wang Aijun shoves the baby back into Lin Meng's arms.

WANG AIJUN
(hissing)
Take your brat and get out. This room isn't free.

LIN XIXI (V.O.)
Ouch. No manners, Grandma.

LIN MENG
(to Wang Aijun)
You can't kick me out—I'm still admitted.

WANG AIJUN
Your chart says discharged. Now.

Wang Aijun pushes Lin Meng toward the door. Lin Xixi begins to CRY again.

LIN MENG
(cradling Xixi)
Okay. We're leaving. We're leaving.

INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - DAY

Lin Meng, barefoot in her hospital gown, carries Lin Xixi through the fluorescent-lit hallway.

LIN XIXI (V.O.)
Don't cry, Mommy. Your love woke me up. Now I'll protect you. We'll be okay.
