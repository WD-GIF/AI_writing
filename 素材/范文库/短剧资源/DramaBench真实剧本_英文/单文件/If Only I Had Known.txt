Title: If Only I Had Known
Description: A woman who neglected her mother for years returns for her birthday, only to discover a tragic accident that changes everything.

EXT. VILLAGE PATH - DAY

Quick cuts: An elderly woman (WANG HUIRU, 70s) falls to the ground, her head hitting a stone. Blood trickles from her temple.

WANG HUIRU
(whispering, disoriented)
Dalong... Xiaoting... the soup...

A college student (JIANG QINGYA, 20s) jogs along the path, stopping short at the sight. She rushes forward, kneeling.

JIANG QINGYA
(gasping)
Auntie Wang! Can you hear me?

Jiang Qingya's fingers tremble as she dials her phone.

WANG HUIRU
(mumbling)
Xiaoting's favorite... red dates...

JIANG QINGYA
(into phone, urgent)
Emergency? Village path east of Willow Creek. Elderly woman, head trauma, unconscious. Yes, immediately!

SIRENS wail in the distance, growing louder.

JIANG QINGYA
(squeezing Wang's hand)
Stay with me. They're coming. Your children need you.

The AMBULANCE screeches to a halt. DR. HU (40s) and a NURSE leap out.

DR. HU
Ma'am, move aside please.

Dr. Hu performs a rapid assessment, his expression grim.

DR. HU
(to Nurse)
GCS 8, possible epidural hematoma. Let's move. Sir, we need family contact.

JIANG QINGYA
I live nearby. I can sign, but please—save her first.

DR. HU
Get in.

INT. SEDAN - DAY

CHEN XIAOTING (35), polished nails gripping the wheel, navigates narrow village roads.

CHEN XIAOTING
(into bluetooth)
Brother, I'm literally five minutes away. Put Mom on, I'll apologize for being late.

CHEN DALONG (V.O.)
She's not answering. It's her birthday, Ting. She waited all morning.

CHEN XIAOTING
(exhaling sharply)
I know. I bought her that coat she wanted. And these cakes from Shanghai—cost a fortune. I'm trying, okay?

She checks her reflection, dabbing lipstick.

CHEN DALONG (V.O.)
Dad died waiting for us to come home. Don't let Mom—

CHEN XIAOTING
(cutting him off)
I'm here now. That's what matters.

The ambulance ROARS around a blind curve, directly toward her.

CHEN XIAOTING
(screaming)
Move! Move!

She jerks the wheel, mounting the embankment. Metal SCREECHES—her mirror rips off, smashing against the ambulance. The vehicle shudders to a halt.

CHEN XIAOTING
(clutching her bleeding hand)
You maniac!

She stumbles out, ready to scream, then freezes.

Through the ambulance's open rear doors, she sees Dr. Hu working frantically. The patient is tiny, frail. A flash of gray hair. A familiar sweater.

Her breath catches. The cake box tumbles from her hands.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - DAY

Chen Xiaoting sprints after the gurney, blood dripping from her hand unnoticed.

CHEN XIAOTING
(panicked)
Mom? Mom! That's my mother!

DR. HU
(stern)
Wait here.

The double doors SLAM shut. Chen Dalong (40s) rushes in, breathless.

CHEN DALONG
Mom? Where is she? What happened?

CHEN XIAOTING
(whispering)
I saw her, Dalong. I saw her on that stretcher. The blood...

Her legs give way. He catches her.

CHEN DALONG
(to reception)
Our mother—Wang Huiru—just arrived. I'm her son.

RECEPTIONIST
Sir, the doctor needs consent for emergency craniotomy. Are you immediate family?

CHEN XIAOTING
(voice cracking)
I'm her daughter. I'll sign. I'll sign anything.

She grabs the forms, her designer handbag slipping from her shoulder.

INT. HOSPITAL WAITING ROOM - NIGHT

Four hours later. The brothersister sit in silence. Chen Xiaoting's nails are chipped, her coat stained with her own blood.

DR. HU enters, surgical cap in hand. They spring up.

DR. HU
She's stable. We relieved the pressure. But...

He pauses.

DR. HU (CONT'D)
Mrs. Wang signed a DNR three years ago. She has extensive cerebral hemorrhage. Even if she wakes, quality of life...

CHEN XIAOTING
(swaying)
Three years ago? When Dad died?

DR. HU
(nods)
She didn't want to burden her children. Said you'd moved to the city, had important lives.

Chen Xiaoting stumbles to a chair, the words a physical blow.

CHEN DALONG
(quietly)
We haven't been home in three years, Ting. Three birthdays we missed.

INT. HOSPITAL ROOM - NIGHT

Wang Huiru lies unconscious, monitors beeping softly. Chen Xiaoting sits at her bedside, holding her weathered hand.

CHEN XIAOTING
(trembling)
Mom... it's me. Xiaoting.

She produces the cake box, crushed and smeared.

CHEN XIAOTING (CONT'D)
I brought your favorite. Red bean. Just like you used to make.

A tear falls on their joined hands.

CHEN XIAOTING (CONT'D)
You called my name. You were waiting for me. And I... I almost hit the ambulance.

She reaches into her bag, pulling out a designer coat.

CHEN XIAOTING (CONT'D)
This? This doesn't matter. None of it matters.

She buries her face in her mother's arm.

CHEN XIAOTING (CONT'D)
Please. Just one more chance. Let me make the soup this time. Let me...

EXT. VILLAGE HOUSE - DAY (FLASHBACK)

A younger Wang Huiru stirs a pot, Chen Xiaoting (15) beside her.

WANG HUIRU
When I'm old, will you still make this for me?

YOUNG CHEN XIAOTING
Every single birthday, Mom. Promise.

INT. HOSPITAL ROOM - NIGHT

Chen Xiaoting looks at her mother, then at her own manicured hands. She begins picking at the nail polish, each chip a revelation.

CHEN XIAOTING
(to herself)
Every... single... birthday.

She curls up beside her mother, finally home.
