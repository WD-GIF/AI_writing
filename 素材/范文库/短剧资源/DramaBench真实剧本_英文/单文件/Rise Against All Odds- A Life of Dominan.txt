# === 剧本前半 (Fountain 格式) ===

Title: Rise Against All Odds: A Life of Dominance
Description: A young man wrongfully imprisoned for murder discovers his parents were killed while trying to clear his name, and a mysterious inmate offers him a chance to escape and seek revenge.

INT. PRISON SOLITARY CONFINEMENT CELL - DAY

A SIGN on the wall reads: "SOLITARY CONFINEMENT AREA."

CHEN XUEWEN, a young prisoner in a torn uniform with healing injuries, sits motionless against the concrete. The food slot opens.

DU LAO, a middle-aged prisoner with glasses, peers through.

DU LAO
Your parents stopped coming. Three weeks ago.

CHEN XUEWEN
(voice hollow)
They gave up.

DU LAO
Or someone made them stop. Forever.

Chen jerks toward the bars.

CHEN XUEWEN
What are you saying?

DU LAO
They were coming to see me. About your case. Then they died. Car accident.

CHEN XUEWEN
(quietly)
That's what I was told.

DU LAO
In Black Water, accident and execution look the same on paper. You want truth? Stay alive. Ghosts don't get answers.

The slot slams shut.

CUT TO:

FLASHBACK - ONE MONTH AGO

INT. PRISON CORRIDOR - DAY

GUARDS shove CHEN XUEWEN through intake. He's clean-cut, terrified.

CHEN XUEWEN
(shouting)
Officer! The timeline doesn't match—check the library cameras!

GUARD #1
Save it for your appeal, kid.

They throw him into GENERAL POPULATION. The door CLANGS shut. SCARFACE, prisoner leader with a scar, watches from his bunk.

SCARFACE
Fresh meat thinks he's special.

Chen is knocked to the ground.

INT. PRISON CELL - DAY

Prisoners surround newcomer CHEN XUEWEN.

PRISONER A
Kid.

PRISONER B
I heard you're in here for raping and killing a college girl. That true?

CHEN XUEWEN
(terrified)
No—I was at the library—

PRISONER B
(sneering)
Sure.

SCARFACE steps forward. Punches Chen in the stomach. Chen doubles over.

CHEN XUEWEN
(gasping)
Coward...

SCARFACE
(contemptuously)
You talk back?

SCARFACE kicks him down.

SCARFACE
Beat him until he learns.

Prisoners rush forward. DU LAO watches coldly from his bunk.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. PRISON CAFETERIA - DAY

Prisoners line up for food. CHEN XUEWEN, thinner now, approaches TRUSTEE GUARD RAMOS.

CHEN XUEWEN
Sir. My parents visited every other day. Now nothing. Please—can you check?

RAMOS
(avoiding eye contact)
Stop talking. Eat your food.

RAMOS moves off. Chen stands frozen. Behind him, OLD MAN WU (70s), a lifer, murmurs.

OLD MAN WU
They won't tell you official. But word travels. Car accident. Both of them. Last Tuesday.

The food tray falls. Spills across the floor.

CHEN XUEWEN
(repeating)
My parents... are dead?

He stares at the mess. His hands shake—not with grief. With something colder.

SCARFACE and followers approach, surrounding Chen.

SCARFACE
(mocking)
College boy's learning his place. On the floor.

SCARFACE spits on the spilled food.

SCARFACE
Show us how sorry you are.

Prisoners laugh. Chen raises his head. His eyes are different now—empty of fear.

CHEN XUEWEN
(quietly)
My parents are dead because of you people.

SCARFACE
What'd you say?

Chen's hand darts out—grabs a broken shard. He launches at Scarface, drives him onto a table, shard at his throat.

SCARFACE
(terrified)
I'm talking to you! Can't you hear me!

CHEN XUEWEN
(roaring)
YOU THINK I'M EASY TO BULLY! FRAMING ME, KILLING THEM! I'M ALREADY DEAD—BUT I'M TAKING YOU!

PRISON WARDEN BASONG arrives with guards.

BASONG
(shouting)
STAND DOWN!

Guards rush forward. Tasers crackle. Chen convulses. They drag him off. Through chaos, DU LAO watches.

CHEN XUEWEN
(struggling)
Let me die! Let me die!

DU LAO
(to a guard)
I'll calm him.

Du Lao locks Chen in a chokehold. Whispers.

DU LAO
(whispering)
Not here. Not like this.

Chen goes limp.

GUARD
Take him to the hole!

INT. PRISON SOLITARY CONFINEMENT CELL - NIGHT

CHEN XUEWEN curls up in the corner, covered in fresh wounds, eyes vacant.

CHEN XUEWEN
(muttering)
Mom... Dad... I'm coming...

DU LAO appears at the cell door holding a broom and dustpan. He unlocks it.

DU LAO
Young man. Three days without food. You keep this up, you'll die. And no one will know why your parents were murdered.

CHEN XUEWEN suddenly looks up. Grabs the bars.

CHEN XUEWEN
(urgently)
What do you know?

DU LAO
I know they were silenced. I know who gave the order. And I know you have a choice.

(beat)

Become a ghost or become a weapon. But you can't be both.

FADE OUT.
