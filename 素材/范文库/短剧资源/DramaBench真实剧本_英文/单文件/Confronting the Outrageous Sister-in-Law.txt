Title: Confronting the Outrageous Sister-in-Law
Description: A family faces an unexpected confrontation when a woman claims their son got her pregnant, but flashbacks reveal the demanding nature of the son's girlfriend during their first meeting.

INT. QIAO FAMILY LIVING ROOM - DAY

Violent banging sounds come from outside the door.

BAI ZHE (O.S.)
Everyone in the Qiao family, get the hell out here!

QIAO KELAN hears the noise and walks toward the door, looking puzzled.

QIAO KELAN
Who's making such a racket?

QIAO KELAN opens the door. BAI ZHE barges in with a group of people, pushing past her.

QIAO KELAN
What do you think you're doing?

QIAO FATHER and QIAO MOTHER emerge from the inner room.

QIAO FATHER
This is private property—who are you?

BAI MOTHER walks in, followed by BAI LU, who is visibly pregnant.

BAI MOTHER
Witness this outrage! The Qiao family's son got my daughter pregnant, and now he refuses to take responsibility! What kind of monsters are you?

QIAO MOTHER turns to her son QIAO MINGWEI, who's just appeared, her face pale.

QIAO MOTHER
Mingwei... please tell me this isn't true.

QIAO MINGWEI
Mom, it's complicated—

BAI ZHE
Complicated? My sister's been carrying your child for six months while you ghosted her!

QIAO KELAN steps forward, her voice steady.

QIAO KELAN
Please, everyone, let's discuss this civilly. If my brother is truly the father, we'll do right by your family. Miss Bai—

BAI LU
(coldly)
It's Lulu. That's what Mingwei called me when we were together.

QIAO KELAN
(faint recognition crossing her face)
Lulu. Of course.

BAI MOTHER
Don't try any tricks! My daughter's reputation is ruined!

CUT TO:


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. QIAO FAMILY LIVING ROOM - DAY (FLASHBACK)

QIAO KELAN, QIAO FATHER, and QIAO MOTHER sit on the sofa in a warm atmosphere. QIAO KELAN is styling her mother's hair.

QIAO MOTHER
(smiling at her reflection)
You always make me look ten years younger.

QIAO MOTHER
Kelan, Mingwei will be here with his girlfriend any minute. Did you prepare the hongbao?

QIAO KELAN
It's ready, Mom. Your three thousand is inside, and I added more to make it ten thousand and one—symbolizing that Mingwei found a one-in-ten-thousand girl. Everything's perfect.

QIAO FATHER
You shouldn't spend your money on this.

QIAO KELAN
Dad, Mingwei is my brother. What's mine is his. It's nothing.

The door opens. QIAO MINGWEI enters with LULU, who wears a designer handbag and assesses the room with barely concealed scrutiny.

QIAO MINGWEI
Dad, Mom—this is Bai Lu. Lulu, my sister Kelan.

QIAO KELAN
(warmly extending her hand)
Lulu, Mingwei's told us so much about you.

LULU
(vague smile, ignoring the hand)
Uncle, Auntie, I've been looking forward to meeting you. Mingwei says you live in the old district?

QIAO FATHER
Please, sit down.

They sit. LULU perches on the edge of the sofa, eyeing the furniture.

QIAO MOTHER
Kelan, the gift.

QIAO KELAN
Of course.

QIAO KELAN hands the red envelope to her mother.

QIAO MOTHER
(presenting it to LULU)
Lulu, a small token. We hope you'll feel welcomed into our family.

LULU accepts the envelope, feeling its weight. Her smile vanishes. She sets it on the table untouched.

LULU
(quietly, to MINGWEI)
Mingwei, did I do something wrong?

QIAO MINGWEI
What? No, why?

LULU
(tears welling)
Then why this... this insult? Back home, a first meeting gift starts at eighty-eight thousand. How can I show my face?

QIAO MOTHER
Eighty-eight—?

QIAO KELAN
(gently)
Lulu, customs vary. That's nearly a bride price in our city. Perhaps there's been a misunderstanding.

LULU
(standing)
There's no misunderstanding. Either your family respects me, or... or we have nothing more to discuss.

She heads toward the door. QIAO MINGWEI scrambles after her.

CUT TO:

INT. QIAO FAMILY LIVING ROOM - DAY

QIAO KELAN faces BAI LU, recognition hardening into resolve.

QIAO KELAN
Lulu, I remember now. And I remember you walked out that day over money. Before we discuss "responsibility," let's discuss paternity tests and why a woman who demanded eighty-eight thousand to exist in our lives is now demanding entry.

BAI LU's face drains of color. BAI ZHE looks between them, confused.

BAI ZHE
What are you talking about?

QIAO KELAN
I'm talking about a pattern. You want Qiao family money? You'll get Qiao family justice first.

QIAO MOTHER
Kelan's right. We Qiaos take responsibility—but not for lies.

BAI LU
(quietly)
The baby is Mingwei's. I swear—

QIAO KELAN
Then you'll have no objection to proving it. And to explaining why you disappeared for six months only to return with demands.

She pulls out her phone.

QIAO KELAN
Shall I call the hospital, or would you prefer to leave peacefully?

The silence stretches. BAI LU's hands tremble on her belly.

BAI LU
(whispering)
I... I need to think.

She turns and hurries out, her family following. The door slams. QIAO KELAN turns to her shell-shocked family.

QIAO KELAN
Mom, Dad, Mingwei—whatever this is, we'll face it together. But on our terms.

She puts a steadying hand on MINGWEI's shoulder as we—

FADE OUT.
