Title: Mother-in-Law's Card Game Saves the Day
Description: A skilled card player mother-in-law wins at every table, but rushes to save her son-in-law when he's trapped in a gambling den after being set up.

INT. COURTYARD MAHJONG TABLE - DAY

Four women huddle around a crowded mahjong table in a tight community courtyard. QIN LING (60s, sharp-eyed, nimble fingers) casually arranges her tiles. Three NEIGHBORS sweat nervously.

MALE CARD PLAYER
(leaning over Aunt Zhang's shoulder)
Hey, let me draw for you. Can't be worse than your luck.

AUNT ZHANG
(praying with hands together)
God of Wealth, just once. Let me break even before my husband notices.

CARD PLAYER A
(impatiently)
Play something. Anything.

QIN LING
(smiling faintly)
Last tile.

QIN LING reveals her hand with a quiet flourish.

QIN LING
Nine Gates. Pure and perfect.

CARD PLAYER B
(awestruck)
Twenty years... first time I've seen a Nine Gates in the wild.

AUNT ZHANG
(defeated)
That's six rounds straight. You should just empty my purse at the start.

MALE CARD PLAYER
(to others)
Told you. Never bet against Qin Ling.

QIN LING
(gathering cash with practiced ease)
Sun's getting low. My daughter's family is coming for dinner.

AUNT ZHANG
(grabbing her sleeve)
One more round. Double stakes.

QIN LING
(standing firmly)
When I'm good, I'm good. When I'm done, I'm done.

CARD PLAYER B
(to the table)
Wins and walks. Every. Single. Time.

INT. COURTYARD PAIGOW TABLE - DAY

QIN LING crosses the courtyard to a rougher table where four MEN play Pai Gow. They stiffen as she approaches.

CARD PLAYER C
(covering his tiles)
Sister Qin. We weren't expecting royalty.

CARD PLAYER D
(scrambling)
Go, go, deal her in.

CARD PLAYER C
(sighing)
I'm out. Folded before I started.

QIN LING
(sitting smoothly)
Don't be dramatic. One hand.

CARD PLAYER E
(resigned)
There goes my cigarette money for the month.

The DEALER slides tiles. QIN LING's fingers dance across them.

CARD PLAYER F
(confident)
Double nine cakes. Beat that, Sister Qin.

QIN LING
(laughing softly)
Let me see.

She reveals the Supreme Pair with a sharp snap.

CARD PLAYER C
(jumping back)
Supreme Pair?! We're playing for pennies!

CARD PLAYER E
(to the ceiling)
Why do I do this to myself?

QIN LING
(collecting the modest pot)
Gentlemen, it's been educational.

She stands, leaving them gaping.

CARD PLAYER F
That's it? One hand?

CARD PLAYER D
She's a phantom. A card shark phantom.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. INDOOR CARD TABLE - DAY

QIN LING enters a smoky back room. XIAO LIU (20s, cocky) deals cards to three PLAYERS.

XIAO LIU
(slapping cards down)
Today I bankrupt you grandpas.

QIN LING
(leaning in the doorway)
Mind if I cut in, Xiao Liu?

XIAO LIU
(stuttering)
A-Aunt Qin. I meant, please, sit.

CARD PLAYER H
(awed)
You're everywhere.

CARD PLAYER I
(chuckling)
Like a tax collector.

QIN LING
(settling in)
Only for the careless.

She plays with surgical precision.

QIN LING
Bomb. Straight. Triple with kicker. Spring hand. Pay.

The table groans. She sweeps the money.

EXT. COURTYARD - DAY

QINGQING (30s, frantic) skids her bicycle into the courtyard, spots QIN LING emerging.

QINGQING
(gasping for breath)
Aunt Qin! Da Niu's in trouble. They dragged him to the—

QIN LING
(calm exterior cracking)
My son-in-law doesn't gamble.

QINGQING
Exactly! He refused, so they forced him. Said he owes "entertainment fees." Now he's down five thousand at Boss Zhou's den.

QIN LING
(eyes flashing)
Get me there. Now.

INT. GAMBLING DEN - DAY

Smoke hangs thick. DA NIU (40s, terrified) sits at a dice table. His shirt is soaked with sweat and spilled beer. A HEAVY (30s, scarred) looms behind him.

GAMBLERS
Big! Big! Big!

The dice settle. Wrong. DA NIU watches his last chips vanish.

DA NIU
(whispering)
It's gone. My daughter's tuition...

EXT. OUTSIDE GAMBLING DEN - DAY

QINGQING's bicycle screeches to a halt. A NEIGHBOR woman waits, twisted with worry.

FEMALE NEIGHBOR
Sister Qin, thank heaven. They're not letting him leave. Says he owes interest now.

QIN LING
(striding forward)
I brought interest.

She pulls a thick roll of cash from her pocket.

INT. GAMBLING DEN - CONTINUOUS

QIN LING bursts through the door, QINGQING behind her. The room goes silent.

QIN LING
(to the room)
My son-in-law's debt. I'll clear it.

BOSS ZHOU (50s, gold chains) emerges from shadows.

BOSS ZHOU
(smiling coldly)
A woman? How quaint. Double or nothing. Your money against his freedom.

QIN LING
(fierce smile)
One hand. My game.

She points to a pristine mahjong table in the corner.

QIN LING
Nine Gates. If I don't make it, you get everything. If I do, Da Niu walks.

The room holds its breath.
