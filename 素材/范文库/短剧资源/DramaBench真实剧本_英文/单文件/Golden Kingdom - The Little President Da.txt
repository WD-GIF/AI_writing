TITLE CARD: GOLDEN KINGDOM - THE LITTLE PRESIDENT DARES TO LOVE ME

EXT. BAI FAMILY VILLA - DAY

A light drizzle. BAI QIAN (24, sharp eyes hidden beneath calculated nonchalance) stands with her back to the gates, phone pressed to her ear.

BAI QIAN
(into phone)
I don't care what it takes. Get me everything on Ouyang Feng's real estate holdings. Not the family—the man himself.

A MAID rushes through the rain, umbrella tilted against the wind.

MAID
(out of breath)
Young Miss, Fifth Madam moved up the meeting. She's already inside. Brought accountants.

BAI QIAN
(smiles, hangs up)
Here comes the real trouble.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. BAI FAMILY LIVING ROOM - DAY

LIU WU (60s, silk suit, jade bracelet clicking with every gesture) pours tea with ceremonial precision. BAI QIAN watches from the doorway.

LIU WU
(without turning)
Your grandfather's will was quite specific, Qian'er. The gold coin must be transferred before the new moon.

BAI QIAN
(enters, sits opposite)
The Ouyang coin belongs to the main family line. Not some fifth concubine's gambling debt.

LIU WU
(slides a velvet box across the table)
The patriarch's seal says otherwise. Young Master Ouyang has already accepted the alliance.

Inside the box: a single ancient gold coin, stamped with a phoenix.

LIU WU (CONT'D)
(leaning forward)
Of course, a man of his stature would never bind himself to... damaged goods. Make him see that.

BAI QIAN
(quietly)
And if I refuse?

LIU WU
Then I'll activate the secondary clause. Your sister gets your mother's medical bills. All of them.

BAI QIAN's fingers tighten on the box. She stands.

BAI QIAN
(bright smile)
For my family's happiness, I'll make sure this engagement dies a swift death.

MONTAGE

EXT. COUNTRY ROAD - DAY

BAI QIAN weaves on a pink electric scooter, Hello Kitty helmet, fake designer bag. She stops to buy oranges from a roadside vendor.

BAI QIAN (V.O.)
Step one: Become the nightmare they never saw coming.

The vendor hands her change. She counts it twice, loudly.

EXT. OUYANG FAMILY VILLA ENTRANCE - DAY

The gates tower like a fortress. OUYANG FENG (28, quiet intensity) stands at the top step, watching the scooter's approach through security cameras.

OUYANG FENG
(to his assistant, softly)
She's wearing last season's Chanel. Badly replicated. Interesting.

SHANGGUAN YAN (26, ice in her veins) joins him, spotting the scooter.

SHANGGUAN YAN
Is that... her? Feng, this is beneath you.

OUYANG FENG
Let's see how deep the performance goes.

BAI QIAN screeches to a halt, knocking over a potted bonsai.

BAI QIAN
(overly loud)
Oopsie! I'll pay for that. With my future husband's money, 'kay?

The HEAD MAID hurries forward with a velvet cushion for the gold coin ceremony.

HEAD MAID
Please present your family's coin, Miss Bai—

SHANGGUAN YAN
(intercepts)
Don't you dare. This is a sacred exchange, not a farmer's market.

She swats at BAI QIAN's orange bag. BAI QIAN lets it fall—strategically. Oranges tumble down the marble steps.

BAI QIAN
(faux gasp)
My dowry! Do you know how much these cost? Six yuan a jin!

SHANGGUAN YAN
(whispers harshly)
You're trash. My family earned our coin through blood at Chang'an. You—

OUYANG FENG
(cuts in, voice low)
Yan'er. The Ouyang code. Article three.

SHANGGUAN YAN's face drains. She knows what's coming.

OUYANG FENG (CONT'D)
(to BAI QIAN, formally)
My cousin dishonored your offering. Choose her penalty.

BAI QIAN
(crouches, picks up a muddy orange)
Just have her taste my sincerity.

OUYANG FENG
(nods)
Peel it. Yourself.

SHANGGUAN YAN
Feng—

OUYANG FENG
Now.

The servants turn away. SHANGGUAN YAN's hands shake as she peels the orange, dirt under her nails. Takes one bite. Tears of rage.

SHANGGUAN YAN
(quietly)
Delicious.

BAI QIAN
(leans in, whisper)
The bitterness hides in the pith. Like family.

OUYANG FENG
(observing, to himself)
She doesn't flinch. Not even when Yan'er raised her hand.

BAI QIAN
(stands, stretches)
So, when's the wedding? I'll need an advance.

OUYANG FENG
You'll move in tonight. East wing.

BAI QIAN
Whoa, whoa. A million a month, or I spill family secrets to the press. I've got pics of your grandfather's mistress.

SHANGGUAN YAN
(laughs, recovering)
She's blackmailing you! Feng, throw her out!

OUYANG FENG
(looks directly at BAI QIAN for the first time)
Two million. Starting tonight. But you'll earn it.

He descends the steps, stops beside her.

OUYANG FENG (CONT'D)
(softly, for her ears only)
The replica Chanel is a nice touch. The real one doesn't use that clasp.

He walks away. BAI QIAN's smile freezes.

BAI QIAN (V.O.)
(quiet panic)
He knew. He knew from the first second.

FADE OUT.
