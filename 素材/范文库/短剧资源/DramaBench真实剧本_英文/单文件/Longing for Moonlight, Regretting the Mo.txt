# === 剧本前半 (Fountain 格式) ===

Title: Longing for Moonlight, Regretting the Morning Sun
Description: A man who gave everything to his beloved fiancée discovers her betrayal the night before their wedding, only to be reborn and given a second chance.

INT. CAR - NIGHT

LIN ZE (V.O.)
They say love makes you blind. I built my whole world around Xu Ruoxi—gave her everything she asked for, and everything she didn't. Tomorrow, we're supposed to get married.

LIN ZE sits in the driver's seat, looking at a photo of XU RUOXI on his phone. He dials her number. It rings four times, then voicemail.

INT. KTV - NIGHT

A private room filled with laughter. XU RUOXI sits among friends, the light catching the diamond on her finger.

CUI YUANYUAN
To Ruoxi! May your last night of freedom be unforgettable.

ALL raise their glasses, the moment genuine.

FRIEND A
That ring is stunning. Lin Ze has taste.

CUI YUANYUAN
(gently)
He commissioned it months ago. Custom design—apparently cost a small fortune.

XU RUOXI
(small smile)
He's... generous.

Her phone BUZZES on the table. "Lin Ze" lights up the screen.

INT. CAR - NIGHT

LIN ZE (V.O.)
Generous. That's one word for it. I thought love meant giving until it hurts. I didn't know she'd be taking from someone else too.

INT. KTV - NIGHT

The party continues. A distinguished man, MENG TIANHAO, enters quietly. All eyes turn to him.

CUI YUANYUAN
(leaning to Ruoxi)
You didn't tell me he was back.

XU RUOXI stands, surprise and something else flickering across her face.

MENG TIANHAO
(raising his glass)
To your happiness, Ruoxi. I mean that.

XU RUOXI
Senior... I thought you were still in Berlin.

MENG TIANHAO
I was. But some things couldn't wait.

Their eyes hold. Her phone BUZZES again—Lin Ze. She silences it without looking.

MENG TIANHAO
You should answer. He'll worry.

XU RUOXI
(quietly)
He always worries.

She walks to the corner, answers.

INT. CAR / KTV - NIGHT (INTERCUT)

LIN ZE
Ruoxi? The office said you left hours ago.

XU RUOXI
I'm still in meetings. Can we not do this tonight?

LIN ZE
(slight pause)
You never miss calls. Not three in a row.

XU RUOXI
(defensive)
I'm trying to respect my team. Is that so hard to understand?

LIN ZE
I understand more than you think.

The line goes dead. Xu Ruoxi stares at her phone, a flicker of something like guilt crossing her face.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. XU FAMILY HOME - DAY (FLASHBACK)

LIN ZE, wearing a suit, sits with XU RUOXI's parents. The atmosphere is businesslike.

ZHANG YAFANG
We want our daughter's future secured, Lin Ze. The ten million is... insurance. As is the property. You understand.

LIN ZE
I want Ruoxi to have everything she deserves.

He places a bank card on the table.

ZHANG YAFANG
(avoiding his eyes)
We... don't have much to offer in return. Family circumstances, you see.

LIN ZE
I didn't expect anything. I love your daughter.

XU RUOXI
(from doorway)
Mother, that's enough. Lin Ze, we have a conference call with your board in twenty minutes. Let's go.

Her voice is chilly, but her eyes don't quite meet his.

INT. LIN ZE'S HOME - DAY (FLASHBACK)

LIN ZE holds financial statements, his hands shaking slightly.

LIN ZE
Ruoxi, thirty million was moved from the subsidiary yesterday. Without board approval. Your signature is on it.

XU RUOXI
(pouring coffee)
Senior Meng's biotech firm needs bridge financing. He's good for it.

LIN ZE
He's been "good for it" for three years. Our investors are asking questions.

XU RUOXI
And I'm answering them. I'm still CFO, aren't I? While your father's in the hospital, someone has to make decisions.

LIN ZE
Not like this. Not behind my back.

XU RUOXI
(turning)
Everything I do is for this family. For us. You'd see that if you weren't so busy playing the wounded husband.

INT. LIN ZE'S HOME - DAY (LATER)

LIN ZE sits in silence, the divorce papers on the coffee table before him. The house feels hollow.

LIN ZE
Ten years. I built this life for you.

XU RUOXI
I never asked you to. I needed security. You needed... what? A trophy? A project? I was never just your wife, Lin Ze. I was your acquisition.

MENG TIANHAO (O.S.)
And a valuable one.

MENG TIANHAO enters, carrying a briefcase.

MENG TIANHAO
The transfer documents are signed. With Ruoxi's shares and proxy votes from her family, I'm now the majority stakeholder. This house, the company—they're mine.

LIN ZE
(voice hollow)
You were planning this from the beginning.

XU RUOXI
From the day we met. Senior saw the opportunity. I just... stopped fighting it.

LIN ZE
Why tell me now?

XU RUOXI
Because I want you to know it was never personal. It was always business.

LIN ZE stares at them both. Then, very slowly, he picks up a pen. His hand trembles as he signs. The moment the last stroke is complete, he goes very still. Then, without a word, he stands and walks toward the door.

MENG TIANHAO
Lin Ze—

LIN ZE stops, but doesn't turn.

LIN ZE
(quietly)
You were wrong, Ruoxi. It was always personal to me.

He walks out. The door clicks shut. Xu Ruoxi looks at the signed papers, and for the first time, something like doubt crosses her face.

INT. CAR - NIGHT (PRESENT)

LIN ZE gasps, gripping the steering wheel. The memory—so vivid, so real—fades. He touches his chest where the phantom pain still echoes.

LIN ZE (V.O.)
That life ended with a door closing. This one begins with one opening. I don't know how or why I'm back here. But I know one thing: the man who walked out of that house doesn't exist anymore.

He steps out of the car, his movements deliberate.

INT. KTV - NIGHT (PRESENT)

XU RUOXI and MENG TIANHAO sit apart, the distance between them charged.

XU RUOXI
You shouldn't have come back. Tomorrow, I'll be his wife in name.

MENG TIANHAO
And in truth?

XU RUOXI
(quietly)
The truth doesn't matter anymore. It died when my father lost everything in '08 and we had to rebuild on Lin Ze's charity.

MENG TIANHAO
This isn't charity, Ruoxi. This is strategy. And you're the key.

He leans closer. The crowd begins chanting.

FRIENDS
Kiss! Kiss! Kiss!

MENG TIANHAO's hand touches her face. Xu Ruoxi closes her eyes, conflict written across her features. Their lips are inches apart when—

The door opens. LIN ZE stands there, not the broken man they remember, but someone harder. His eyes lock with Xu Ruoxi's, then drift to Meng Tianhao.

The music continues, but the room goes silent.

XU RUOXI
(whisper)
Lin Ze...

LIN ZE
(quietly, dangerously)
Don't stop on my account. After all, it's just business, right?

FADE OUT.
