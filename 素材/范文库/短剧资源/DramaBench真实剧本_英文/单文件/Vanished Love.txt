Title: Vanished Love
Description: A mother rushes her sick son to the hospital on her daughter's birthday, only to face her husband's fury and a devastating choice between her two children.

EXT. HOSPITAL - DAY

A series of fast-paced hospital shots: a nurse pouring saline solution, hospital bed wheels rolling rapidly, ambulance lights flashing, surgical instruments, blood bags.

NURSE (V.O.)
Incoming acute renal failure. Six-year-old male, status post. Prep the pediatric ICU team now!

INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - DAY

ZHOU WENMEI, wearing a green dress, runs anxiously through the hospital corridor.

ZHOU WENMEI
Mingzhe, thank God—

SU MINGZHE shoves ZHOU WENMEI against the wall, his face contorted with panic.

SU MINGZHE
(low, dangerous)
Dr. Zhao said one more episode could kill him. One. And you took him to an amusement park?

ZHOU WENMEI
Tears streaming down her face, she reaches for him.

ZHOU WENMEI
(struggling for breath)
Today is Yiyi's birthday. Xiaohui begged me. He wanted to feel normal, just for one hour—

SU MINGZHE
(fingers digging into her shoulders)
Normal? My son will never be normal because you decided to have another child. The pregnancy complications weakened him. Every specialist said so.

ZHOU WENMEI
That's not how—

SU MINGZHE
(shoving her away)
Since Yiyi was born, it's been infection after infection. The fortune teller was right. She's poisoned this family.

ZHOU WENMEI
(on her knees)
She's four years old. Your daughter.

SU MINGZHE
My son is dying. My company is collapsing from his medical bills. She took everything from him before she could even walk.

ZHOU WENMEI
(crawling forward)
Mingzhe, please. I know you blame me. But they're both my babies. I can't choose—

SU MINGZHE
(turning away)
Then I'll choose for you. Where is she? I told Song to bring her.

ZHOU WENMEI
(panicking)
Yiyi was right behind me in the lobby. She promised she'd stay with the receptionist—

SONG YUANJIANG rushes over, his expression grim.

SONG YUANJIANG
Mr. Su, the receptionist says she never arrived at the desk.

ZHOU WENMEI
(frantic)
What? No, I told her "wait by the flowers, don't move"—

FLASHBACK

EXT. HOSPITAL ENTRANCE - DAY

ON SCREEN: Twenty minutes ago

ZHOU WENMEI carries XIAOHUI toward the sliding doors, YIYI clutching her dress.

ZHOU WENMEI
Yiyi, wait by the flower pots. Right here. Don't move until I come back.

YIYI
(trembling)
Mommy, I'm scared.

ZHOU WENMEI
(fighting tears)
Be my brave girl. Just for a little while.

INT. HOSPITAL LOBBY - DAY

Through the glass doors, we see ZHOU WENMEI sprint toward the ER. YIYI stands alone, watching. A large family rushes past, knocking her small backpack off her shoulders. As she bends to pick it up, she wanders a few steps away from the designated spot. A security guard approaches.

END FLASHBACK

INT. HOSPITAL CORRIDOR - DAY

ZHOU WENMEI
She's lost. Oh God, she's lost in the hospital—

She bolts down the corridor.

INT. HOSPITAL STAIRWELL - DAY

SU MINGZHE and SONG YUANJIANG meet with DR. ZHAO.

DR. ZHAO
Xiaohui's creatinine levels are critical. He needs a transplant within weeks, not months.

SU MINGZHE
The previous compatibility tests—Yiyi was a match?

DR. ZHAO
(hesitant)
She's a 95% match. But Mr. Su, she's under five. The ethics board will never approve harvesting a kidney from a minor without her consent and extensive evaluation.

SU MINGZHE
She can consent. She'll do anything for her brother.

DR. ZHAO
(carefully)
Legally, she cannot. And your wife would need to—

SU MINGZHE
My wife would sign. If it meant saving Xiaohui.

DR. ZHAO
Mrs. Zhou would have to be evaluated for coercion. And Yiyi would need psychological screening. This isn't a simple procedure.

SU MINGZHE
(furious whisper)
My daughter is missing in this hospital while my son codes upstairs. If we find her, and she agrees, will you file the paperwork?

DR. ZHAO
I can start the process. But Mr. Su, the board may still deny it.

SONG YUANJIANG
Sir, security footage shows Yiyi wandering toward the pediatric ward on the fourth floor. Alone.

INT. HOSPITAL WARD - DAY

YIYI sits on a hospital bed, clutching a stuffed rabbit. SU MINGZHE enters, his face transforming from panic to a fragile hope.

SU MINGZHE
(gently)
Found you, little rabbit.

YIYI
Daddy! I got lost. I was trying to find Xiaohui's room.

SU MINGZHE
(sitting beside her)
You want to help your brother?

YIYI
(nodding)
Mommy says he's very sick.

SU MINGZHE
He needs a special gift. From inside you. A tiny bean that makes him better.

YIYI
(scared but brave)
Will it hurt?

SU MINGZHE
A little. But you're so strong. You'd do that for Xiaohui?

YIYI
(wiping her eyes)
If it makes him play with me again.

SU MINGZHE
(hugging her)
Good girl. Such a good girl.

He looks over her head at the NURSE entering.

SU MINGZHE
(to Nurse)
Preliminaries first. Draw her blood. Start the assessment protocol.

The NURSE approaches with a pediatric blood draw kit. YIYI extends her arm, trying to be brave. The rabbit falls from her lap.
