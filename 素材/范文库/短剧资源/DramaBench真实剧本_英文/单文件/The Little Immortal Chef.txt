Title: The Little Immortal Chef
Description: A mischievous immortal girl who inherited the God of Cooking's divine position descends to the mortal world to find her father and fulfill her destiny.

INT. LAKESIDE - DAY

SUPER: IMMORTAL REALM

A little girl in flowing silk robes, YUANYUAN (8), stirs from sleep atop a jade-colored boulder. She stretches, her nose already twitching.

YUANYUAN
(to herself)
Nothing but ambrosia for three hundred years... I'm starving for something real.

She springs up, eyes sparkling with inspiration.

YUANYUAN
Time for something with actual flavor!

Above the misty lake, two divine beasts circle—GOLDEN DRAGON and FIRE PHOENIX—playfully chasing each other through clouds.

MALE VOICE A (V.O.)
The little ancestor's awake! Hide the divine beasts!

MALE VOICE B (V.O.)
Too late—she's already spotted them!

Yuanyuan stands on a floating wooden pier, hands on hips, her laughter like silver bells.

YUANYUAN
Dragon and Phoenix Hot Pot! Perfect!

She whips out a tiny slingshot carved from peach wood. As she aims, the ammunition glows—essence orbs, not stones. She fires once, twice. The divine beasts shimmer and TRANSFORM mid-air into their base spiritual forms: a chicken and a fish, which plop into Yuanyuan's waiting basket.

Yuanyuan catches them effortlessly, already setting up her cooking pot.

INT. COURTYARD - DAY

XUANTIAN LAOZU, an ancient immortal whose white beard trails to his knees, shuffles through the courtyard in panic.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(calling out)
Long'er! Feng'er! Where have those rascals gotten to now?

His nose catches an irresistible aroma. He follows it like a man possessed.

He finds Yuanyuan tending her pot, the broth simmering with heavenly fragrance.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(leaning close to the pot)
Ah, Yuanyuan... that aroma could wake the dead. Mortal fire needs mortal fuel, yes? Let this old one stoke it for you.

XUANTIAN LAOZU casts a spell, and the flames leap up, singeing his sleeve. He doesn't notice.

YUANYUAN
(without looking up)
You just want first taste.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(rubbing his hands together)
A mere sample. For quality assurance.

YUANYUAN
(pointing her spoon)
One spoonful. That's it.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(beaming)
You're a jewel among immortals!

XUANTIAN LAOZU plunges his hand into the pot—time and pain mean nothing to him—and extracts a morsel.

YUANYUAN
(disgusted)
At least use a spoon. We're civilized beings.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(mouth full, eyes wide)
Divine! What is this? I've tasted every beast in the nine heavens, but this...

YUANYUAN
(casually)
Dragon and phoenix, of course.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(shocked)
MY Dragon? MY Phoenix? The ones I've raised since they were eggs?

YUANYUAN
They looked mature enough to harvest.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(grief-stricken)
My beautiful mounts! My companions of a thousand years!

XUANTIAN LAOZU collapses on the ground, beating his chest.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
Little ancestor, please show mercy! The divine beasts on this Spirit Mountain are almost all eaten by you.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT, a fairy in white robes, walks over.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
Yuanyuan. You've consumed half the spiritual guardians of this mountain.

YUANYUAN

# === 剧本后半 ===

(pouting)
It's not my fault. The God of Cooking's divine position requires actual cooking. You can't make an omelet without breaking a few eggs. Or roasting a few dragons.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(standing up)
She has a point. It's her nature.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
Since Yuanyuan loves cooking so much, why don't you go to the mortal world to find your father?

YUANYUAN
(delighted)
Father? I have a father?

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
Every immortal has an origin.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT casts a spell, and an image shimmers in the air—a MAN in a modern chef's coat, his face kind, his hands skilled.

YUANYUAN
(whispering)
He looks... kind. And his hands know work.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
It was a winter's night when your mortal mother saved his life.

FLASHBACK: A young woman tends to an injured man in a mountain inn.

MAN
I thought I'd die alone on that cliff.

WOMAN
Then it's fortunate I was hunting for rare herbs.

END FLASHBACK.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
(noticing YUANYUAN has disappeared)
Hmph, this little imp—

XUANTIAN LAOZU watches as Yuanyuan's life statue in the courtyard—the measure of her divine power—glows, then hardens into ordinary stone.

XUANTIAN LAOZU
(sadly)
She's sealed her immortal power. In the mortal world, she'll be just a gifted child. Until she earns her place.

NINE HEAVENS SAINT
Let us hope gifted is enough.

INT. HOTEL KITCHEN - DAY

The kitchen is bustling. Head Chef ZHANG (40s) towers over LIN YANG (28), a talented sous chef.

CHEF ZHANG
Lin Yang! That prep work should've been done ten minutes ago! This is a kitchen, not a meditation hall!

LIN YANG
Chef Zhang, I'm doing the work of three people here. I can't—

A golden light flashes, and YUANYUAN appears, her ancient robes ludicrously out of place.

She spots Lin Yang's hands—the way they move, the precision in his knife work. Her eyes widen. It's the same hands from the vision.

YUANYUAN
(voice trembling)
Daddy?

Yuanyuan runs to Lin Yang and hugs his leg fiercely.

LIN YANG
(surprised)
Whoa! Hey, little girl. I'm not—

CHEF ZHANG
(exploding)
You bring a KID into my kitchen? Lin Yang, if you don't want to work, get out!

LIN YANG
No, she's not my child.

CHEF ZHANG
If she's not your child, is she mine?

YUANYUAN
(shouting at Chef Zhang)
Don't you bully my daddy!

CHEF ZHANG
So what if I bully him?

YUANYUAN
(pointing at Chef Zhang)
You just wait!

CHEF ZHANG
I'm waiting! Go away!

YUANYUAN walks to the cutting board, picks up a piece of carrot and a cleaver. In a BLUR OF MOTION, she reduces it to perfect julienne. The kitchen falls silent.

LIN YANG and CHEF ZHANG stare, mouths agape.

Yuanyuan looks up, her face fierce with pride and vulnerability.

YUANYUAN
(to Lin Yang)
I can help. Just... don't send me away.
