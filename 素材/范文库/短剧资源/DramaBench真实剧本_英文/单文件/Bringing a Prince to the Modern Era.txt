Title: Bringing a Prince to the Modern Era
Description: A delivery girl accidentally travels to ancient times and brings a prince back to the modern world.

EXT. FILM STUDIO - DAY

On an open field, a yellow electric cart speeds past.

YE ZHAOXI (V.O.)
Two minutes left on the timer.

YE ZHAOXI, a girl wearing a yellow delivery uniform with twin ponytails, stops the cart and grabs the food delivery and her phone. A jade pendant hangs at her neck.

YE ZHAOXI (V.O.)
Last order. Rent's locked.

YE ZHAOXI runs into a large building.

INT. FILM STUDIO CORRIDOR - DAY

YE ZHAOXI sprints through the corridor, passing various actors in costume.

YE ZHAOXI
(dodging through)
Sorry—excuse me!

PASSERBY A
Watch it!

A woman's voice comes from one room.

WOMAN (O.S.)
Dongmei, what are you doing?

In another room, a girl climbs onto a windowsill.

GIRL
Burn, my youth!

YE ZHAOXI continues running forward, passing a simulated airplane cabin set.

FLIGHT ATTENDANT (O.S.)
Ladies and gentlemen, our plane is experiencing turbulence, please remain calm.

INT. AIRPLANE SIMULATION CABIN - DAY

The DIRECTOR sits in front of monitors, watching the flight attendant actress performing.

DIRECTOR
Cut! Who told you to act like that? Can you be more realistic? Do it again!

The DIRECTOR sees YE ZHAOXI at the door.

DIRECTOR
What are you doing?

YE ZHAOXI
I'm delivering food.

DIRECTOR
(impatiently)
Food? Get out!

YE ZHAOXI is driven out. She looks down at her phone.

YE ZHAOXI
(to herself)
Wrong address...

She notices a door marked "HISTORICAL SOUNDSTAGE B-3" and pushes through.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. ANCIENT MILITARY TENT - DAY

The scene switches to an ancient battlefield, two armies at war.

ON SCREEN: 600 BC - KINGDOM OF DANZHU

Inside the military tent, PRINCE DANZHU wears armor, his back to a sand table. He turns around, his eyes resolute.

PRINCE DANZHU
(to the generals)
The Rong tribes raid our borders. We answer with steel. Today, Danzhu writes its legend!

GENERALS
Long live Danzhu! Long live the Prince!

Suddenly, YE ZHAOXI appears out of nowhere in the military tent. She looks around bewildered.

YE ZHAOXI
(quietly)
This is the spot.

The GENERALS and PRINCE DANZHU stare at her in shock.

YE ZHAOXI
(holding up the delivery bag)
Food delivery.

PRINCE DANZHU
Dismissed.

GENERALS
Yes!

The generals leave. Only PRINCE DANZHU and YE ZHAOXI remain in the tent.

YE ZHAOXI
(handing over the food)
Hello, your delivery has arrived. Remember to give me a five-star review.

She looks curiously at the sand table.

YE ZHAOXI
Wow, your crew made this map so detailed, really authentic. Where's the hidden camera? It's really well hidden.

She reaches out to touch PRINCE DANZHU's armor.

PRINCE DANZHU
(grabbing her hand)
Identify yourself. How did you enter this tent?

YE ZHAOXI
(in pain)
Dude, ow ow ow! Do all you actors get this into your roles?

PRINCE DANZHU restrains her, and they end up in an intimate position close together.

PRINCE DANZHU
If you don't answer, you're a spy.

YE ZHAOXI
Just because you're handsome doesn't mean you can do whatever you want! Are you harassing me? Let go!

PRINCE DANZHU
I have ways to make you talk.

INT. ANCIENT MILITARY TENT - CONTINUOUS

A black-clad ASSASSIN suddenly bursts in.

ASSASSIN
Prince Danzhu, die!

The ASSASSIN swings his blade. PRINCE DANZHU immediately shields YE ZHAOXI behind him. As he turns, the jade pendant on YE ZHAOXI's neck emits a brilliant light.

Space-time distorts, and they are both engulfed by the light.

EXT. OUTSIDE FILM STUDIO - DAY

The light fades. PRINCE DANZHU and YE ZHAOXI appear on a modern street. PRINCE DANZHU looks around in shock at the unfamiliar environment.

PRINCE DANZHU
What place is this?

YE ZHAOXI
(impatiently)
Are all the people in your crew like this? All this fighting and killing.

PRINCE DANZHU
Woman, I ask, you answer. How far is this place from Danzhu?

YE ZHAOXI
Danzhu? That was over two thousand years ago.

PRINCE DANZHU
(shocked)
You're saying Danzhu no longer exists?

YE ZHAOXI
Yeah. It's ancient history.

PRINCE DANZHU staggers back, unable to believe it.

PRINCE DANZHU
Two thousand years have already passed...

YE ZHAOXI looks at his devastated expression and freezes.

YE ZHAOXI
No way? You're really the Prince of Danzhu?

They look at each other. The pendant is now dark and lifeless. Both are in extreme shock.
