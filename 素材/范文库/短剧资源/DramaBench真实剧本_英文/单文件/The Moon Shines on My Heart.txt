# === 剧本前半 (Fountain 格式) ===

Title: The Moon Shines on My Heart
Description: A young wife says goodbye to her soldier husband who leaves for war just one month after their wedding. Thirty years later, a chance encounter at a hospital may reunite them.

INT. VILLAGE DIRT ROAD - DAY (THIRTY YEARS AGO)

A military truck loaded with young soldiers slowly drives along. Villagers wave goodbye from the roadside, emotions running high.

Young XIAO YUE (20s) grips the edge of the truck tightly, holding hands with her husband ZHOU GE (20s) on the truck, tears streaming down her face.

ZHOU GE
Xiao Yue, I'm sorry. Just one month after our wedding, I have to leave for the strait war. I've failed you.

XIAO YUE
(crying)
Zhou Ge, you must come back safe and sound.

ZHOU GE
Don't worry. We'll definitely win in six months. Six months from now, wait for me to come home.

The truck accelerates, forcing Xiao Yue to let go. She runs after the truck for a few steps, shouting loudly.

XIAO YUE
(shouting)
Zhou Ge! Zhou Ge! I haven't told you yet - you're going to be a father! It's twins! We'll have both a son and a daughter!

Zhou Ge on the truck leans out as far as he can, but due to the distance and noise, he can't hear clearly.

ZHOU GE
(shouting)
Xiao Yue, what did you say? I can't hear you!

The truck drives away. Xiao Yue stops running, caressing her belly, watching the departing truck, her face covered in tears.

DISSOLVE TO:

INT. XIAO YUE'S HOME - DAY

Elderly XIAO YUE (50s) busies herself in the kitchen, preparing a table full of delicious dishes. She wears a smile, seemingly anticipating something.

Her phone rings. Xiao Yue happily answers.

XIAO YUE
(on phone)
(happy)
Hello?

DAUGHTER XIAO XIAO (V.O.)
Mom? I hate to do this, but my boss just scheduled an emergency meeting for tonight. I tried everything to get out of it--

Xiao Yue's smile falters. She forces brightness into her voice.

XIAO YUE
(on phone)
It's okay, sweetie. Your career is important. We'll celebrate next weekend.

Xiao Yue hangs up, looking at the prepared feast. The phone rings again.

SON NIAN NIAN (V.O.)
Mom, I'm so sorry. Your grandson has a fever and Jun's working late. I can't bring him out in this weather--

XIAO YUE
(on phone)
Take care of my grandson. That's what matters. I'll save you both some mooncakes.

She ends the call, staring at her reflection in the window. Her dog TU DOU nudges her hand.

Later, Xiao Yue takes out a red marriage certificate from a drawer and gently opens it. She caresses the photo of her and Zhou Ge from their youth, then touches the well-worn edges.

She places it beside a framed photo of her children as toddlers, then looks at a newer photo of her grandchildren on the wall.


# === 剧本后半 ===

INT. HOSPITAL LOBBY - DAY

Xiao Yue stands in front of a self-service registration machine, squinting at the screen. Her hands tremble slightly as she touches it.

A YOUNG MAN behind her shifts impatiently.

YOUNG MAN
Can you hurry up?

XIAO YUE
(flustered)
I'm trying... this new system confuses me.

YOUNG MAN
(sighs)
Then get your kids to help! Some of us have actual jobs to get back to.

XIAO YUE
(lowered voice)
They're busy.

She steps aside, clutching her hospital card. The young man brushes past.

Xiao Yue approaches a help desk instead, where a NURSE notices her.

INT. HOSPITAL INFUSION ROOM - DAY

Xiao Yue sits alone in a chair receiving an IV drip. The bag is nearly empty. She watches the drip chamber, tracking each drop.

Across the room, a HOSPITAL MAN (50s) - weathered face, graying hair - also sits with an IV. He glances at her.

Xiao Yue notices him. She stares. His profile, his posture...

Her breath catches. She studies his hands - one bears a faint scar across the knuckles.

FLASHBACK: Zhou Ge's hand, young and strong, that same scar from a childhood accident.

The Hospital Man feels her gaze and turns. Their eyes meet. He blinks, puzzled.

XIAO YUE
(whisper)
Zhou... Ge?

The man tilts his head. A flicker of something crosses his face - confusion, recognition, disbelief.

HOSPITAL MAN
(hoarse)
Do I... know you?

Xiao Yue trembles, tears filling her eyes. She touches her chest, then points to him.

XIAO YUE
It's me. Xiao Yue.

He stares. The IV alarm BEEPS beside him. Neither moves.

A NURSE approaches Xiao Yue.

NURSE
Ma'am, your drip's done.

The Nurse removes Xiao Yue's needle. Xiao Yue doesn't look away from the man.

The Nurse notices the tension, glances between them.

NURSE
(to Hospital Man)
Sir? Your bag's empty too.

He barely nods, still locked on Xiao Yue. The Nurse removes his needle.

When the Nurse leaves, silence stretches between them.

HOSPITAL MAN
(quiet, strained)
They told me... my family thought I was gone. I couldn't remember my name for twenty years. Only fragments.

XIAO YUE
(voice breaking)
We waited. I waited.

He looks down at his weathered hands. When he looks up, his eyes are wet.

HOSPITAL MAN
The twins?

Xiao Yue nods slowly, tears streaming down her face.

XIAO YUE
A son and a daughter. Both grown. Two grandchildren now.

She reaches into her bag with shaking hands and pulls out her phone. Shows him a photo of her family - her children, grandchildren.

His hand trembles as he reaches for the phone.

HOSPITAL MAN
(whisper)
I have a family.

XIAO YUE
You always did.

He hands back the phone. Their fingers brush. A lifetime passes in that touch.

HOSPITAL MAN
I'm staying with... someone who helped me. After the war injuries. But... may I see them? The children?

XIAO YUE
(softly)
They're your children too.

For the first time, a small, hesitant smile touches his lips.

HOSPITAL MAN
Mid-Autumn Festival is next week. I remember... you promised to wait.

XIAO YUE
I never stopped.

He nods, eyes glistening with resolve.

HOSPITAL MAN
Then... we still have time.

Xiao Yue looks at him - this stranger who is her husband. The tears on her face are no longer just of sorrow.

FADE OUT.
