Title: Days to Come
Description: A wealthy businessman returns to his homeland and encounters his former love, now working as a cleaner, who no longer remembers him.

INT. LUXURY CAR - DAY

BO FENGFENG, in a sharp suit and gold-rimmed glasses, stares out the window. SHEN RUOYI, elegant and watchful, sits beside him.

BO FENGFENG
Finally back home.

SHEN RUOYI
Fengfeng, where are we going?

BO FENGFENG
I bought a house here.

EXT. SU FAMILY VILLA - DAY

A black luxury car drives toward a restored mansion.

INT. LUXURY CAR - DAY

SHEN RUOYI sees the villa and tenses.

SHEN RUOYI
The Su family estate?

FLASHBACK:

EXT. BURNING VILLA - NIGHT

Flames consume the mansion. YOUNG BO FENGFENG, desperate, carries an unconscious woman in white (SU QIANQIAN) through smoke.

END FLASHBACK.

SHEN RUOYI
You still can't forget her, can you?

BO FENGFENG
(reminiscing)
Five years ago, after I made my first fortune, I bought this place.

BO FENGFENG
To remind myself what happened here.

SHEN RUOYI
(inner monologue)
Remind yourself... or keep her close?

SHEN RUOYI
(plaintively)
Fengfeng, why can't you look at me?

BO FENGFENG
I'll have the driver take you to the hotel. The banquet's in three days.

SHEN RUOYI takes out a medicine bottle and hands it to BO FENGFENG.

SHEN RUOYI
Your health just recovered. Don't stop taking this.

BO FENGFENG
Okay.

BO FENGFENG gets out.


# === 剧本后半 ===

EXT. VILLA ENTRANCE - DAY

BO FENGFENG adjusts his suit, looking up at the mansion.

FLASHBACK:

EXT. SNOWY FIELD - NIGHT (5 YEARS AGO)

Young BO FENGFENG in a white suit kneels in snow, holding a ring box to SU QIANQIAN, radiant in a winter coat.

BO FENGFENG (V.O.)
Qianqian...

SU QIANQIAN
(turning away)
Don't, Bo. You can't even pay my father's debts. What life can you give me?

EXT. BURNING VILLA - NIGHT (5 YEARS AGO)

The villa erupts in flames. BO FENGFENG charges toward it as SHEN RUOYI shouts.

SHEN RUOYI
Fengfeng!

INT. BURNING VILLA - NIGHT (5 YEARS AGO)

BO FENGFENG finds unconscious SU QIANQIAN, picks her up.

BO FENGFENG
Qianqian, stay with me. I'm getting you out.

He carries her out as glass shatters behind them.

INT. HOSPITAL ROOM - DAY (5 YEARS AGO)

BO FENGFENG wakes with bandaged hands. SHEN RUOYI sits nearby, her own hands bandaged.

BO FENGFENG
Qianqian?

SHEN RUOYI
She's alive. She asked me to tell you something.

BO FENGFENG
What?

SHEN RUOYI
(eyes down)
She said... don't come looking. She doesn't need rescuing.

BO FENGFENG lowers his head. Shen's grip tightens on her own bandages.

END FLASHBACK.

EXT. VILLA ENTRANCE - DAY

Five years later.

BO FENGFENG and a BUTLER walk toward the steps.

INT. VILLA - DAY

Two MAIDS polish the grand staircase.

MAID A
I heard Mr. Bo rebuilt everything exactly as it was. Down to the wallpaper.

MAID B
They say he keeps one room locked. No one's allowed inside.

SU QIANQIAN, in cleaning clothes with worn hands, mops the marble floor, concentrated.

The CLEANING SUPERVISOR approaches.

CLEANING SUPERVISOR
You're on the foyer? Mr. Bo's early. Finish within the hour.

SU QIANQIAN
Okay.

INT. VILLA FOYER - DAY (MOMENTS LATER)

BO FENGFENG enters, stopping short of the wet floor.

SU QIANQIAN
(panicked)
Sorry—I'll move—

She looks up. Their eyes meet. Bo's breath catches. Qianqian's forehead creases with vague recognition.

BO FENGFENG
Qianqian... what are you doing here?

SU QIANQIAN
You know me?

BO FENGFENG
(soft laugh, bitter)
So you really don't remember.

SU QIANQIAN
(cautious)
I think you have the wrong person. I'm just staff.

SU QIANQIAN stands to leave. BO FENGFENG blocks her path.

BO FENGFENG
(low)
Stay.

Qianqian freezes. They stand inches apart.

SU QIANQIAN
(voice small)
Why does your voice sound like tears?

BO FENGFENG
Because I've been waiting five years to ask you a question.

BO FENGFENG opens a door—the one the maids mentioned.

BO FENGFENG
After you see what's inside, you can decide if I'm a stranger.

SU QIANQIAN
(hesitant)
What's in there?

BO FENGFENG
The truth about who you were. And who we were.

He holds out his bandaged hand—the fire scars visible. Qianqian stares at it, something flickering in her eyes.
